## Setup Instructions

<Steps>
1. **Research Your Destination**  
   Understand the culture, language, and lifestyle of the country you wish to study in.

2. **Gather Required Documents**  
   Collect necessary documents such as academic transcripts, letters of recommendation, and a valid passport.

3. **Submit Your Application**  
   Follow the application procedures for your chosen program and keep track of important deadlines.

</Steps>

## Health and Safety Tips

<Aside type="tip">
  Always have your health insurance information accessible and be aware of local emergency contacts.
</Aside>

Regularly check the safety guidelines of your host country and stay informed about local health resources.
